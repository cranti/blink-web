ReSITE

Relative Salience Identified Through Eye-blinks


[App overview] - standalone application, written in MATLAB, that uses eye-blink data to examine the salience of a visual stimulus to a group of viewers. The application runs two kinds of analyses:

Blink Inhibition - determine when a group of viewers is engaged by examining when they are significantly inhibiting their blinking.

Peri-Stimulus Time Histogram - assess the salience of a particular kind of event within a stimulus
